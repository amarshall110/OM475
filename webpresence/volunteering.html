Volunteering

MASCA Pancakes for Poverty Event

In Fall 2018, a group of my peers and I hosted a series of fundraisers in the Barsema Hall Atrium to raise money for the local Hope Haven homeless shelter and resource center. What started off as a project for a course turned into a greater volunteering effort during the semester. We were fortunate enough to garner the support of a local business who helped finance the startup supplies for this event, which included pancake mix, coffee grounds, food containers, and syrup.

During our first major outing of this event, our team was able to raise $219.00 for the Hope Haven Shelter in Dekalb. Aside from the monetary donation, our team was able to donate three full trays of pancakes, two large bottles of syrup, and a tub of butter for the long-term residents to enjoy.
