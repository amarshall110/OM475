Volunteering

MASCA Pancakes for Poverty Event

In Fall 2018, a group of my peers and I hosted a series of fundraisers in the Barsema Hall Atrium to raise money for the local Hope Haven shelter. What started off as a project for a course turned into a greater volunteering effort during the semester. We were fortunate enough to garner the support of a local business who helped finance the startup supplies for this event, which included pancake mix, coffee grounds, food containers, and syrup.
