High School

I attended Reed-Custer High School in Braidwood, Illinois. During my years in High School, I was actively involved in Speech Team, Math Team, Worldwide Youth in Science and Engineering (WYSE), Scholastic Bowl, and soccer. I was also a member of our Concert Band playing bassoon, Jazz Band playing trombone, and musical pit crew playing both instruments depending on the production.

Time Before NIU

After graduating high school in 2010, I immediately entered the University of Illinos at Urbana Champaign, intending to pursue a degree in History. On campus, I was involved in a few university groups, including LAS Leaders, Pancakes for Poverty, and Astrology Club. I soon realized that History was not my passion, and I began taking classes in Physics, Computer Science, and other sciences. No longer knowing what I wanted to pursue as a career, I left the university and began working full time while I worked out my goals. I eventually decided on re-entering university for a degree Business, but unfortunately my Liberal Arts and Sciences and Engineering background did not satisfy many of the early business requirements such as Accounting or Business Law. I took nearby courses at the local Joliet Community College to get some of these requirements out of the way before transferring to NIU.
