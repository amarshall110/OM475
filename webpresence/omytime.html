Brief History

I began attending NIU in the Spring of 2017 as an Accounting Major after taking a variety of classes at another University and a Community College. I knew with certainty that I wanted to be a part of the highly rated College of Business here at NIU, but I was never completely set on Accounting as a long-term profession. After taking the required OMIS (Operations Management and Information Systems) classes as part of the College of Business Curriculum, I realized that the OMIS major played more into my technological strengths and interests.

Over time, I have only fallen more in love with the OMIS major, for it covers a wide variety of topics as part of its core curriculum and helps students develop the tools to sovle real business problems. I have had the opportunity to use and receive training on some of the most prominent business softwares in the industry througout my classes, and I will re-enter the workforce with a strong set of skills that NIU has helped me cultivate over the last two and a half years.

For more information on some of the major projects I have completed, the classes I have taken, or my volunteering project at NIU, please follow the links in the sidebar.
