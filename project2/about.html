About Pressure WashEx

Founding

Pressure WashEx was founded in 2007 by Robert Smith after working in the power washing industry since the early 90s. Robert has worked tirelessly to build a reputation in the field through hard work, honesty, and reliability for all of his customers, whether they be a business owner, owner operator, or home owner seeking power washing services.
