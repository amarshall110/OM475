My Journey to Gaming

Beginning

I was introduced to video games at a very young age by my older brother with the original Sega Genesis system. This hobby has developed across years, genres, and consoles, from 8-bit graphics to interactive Virtual Reality.

Current

While I have played on many different consoles over the years, my current focus has been on the PlayStation 4. This console was released in North America in 2013, and has since had nearly two-thousand games released on the system. Recently, I have been trying my hand at Rainbow Six Siege, a competitive tactics-based shooter game.
