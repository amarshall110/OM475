About Spirited Away

Spirited Away is an animated fantasy film released in 2001. This film was animated by Studio Ghibli, which was founded by the writer and directer Hayou Miyazaki in 1985. Hayou Miyazaki is also known for My Neighbor Totori, Howl's Moving Caste, Kiki's Delivery Service, Princess Mononoke, and many more.
